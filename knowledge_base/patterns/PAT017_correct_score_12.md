# Pattern: Correct Score 1-2 or 2-1

## Expected Hit Rate: 8-12%
## Optimal Odds: 8.00-12.00

## Best Situations
- Competitive matches between similar teams
- Expected goals 2.5-3.0
- Cup matches (more open)

## Value Finding
- When total goals odds suggest 2.5
- When BTTS is likely
- When neither team keeps clean sheets
