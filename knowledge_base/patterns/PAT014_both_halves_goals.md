# Pattern: Goals in Both Halves

## Win Rate: 55-62%
## Optimal Odds: 1.70-1.90

## Logic
- Most matches have goals spread across halves
- First half tends to have fewer (cautious start)
- Second half more open

## Entry Criteria
- Expected total goals > 2.5
- Both teams capable of scoring
- Not a defensive matchup
- High-scoring league
