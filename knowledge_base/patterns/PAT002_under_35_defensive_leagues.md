# Pattern: Under 3.5 Goals in Defensive Leagues

## Win Rate: 75-77%
## Optimal Odds: 1.45-1.55

## Target Leagues
| League | Under 3.5 Rate |
|--------|----------------|
| Championship | 76.0% |
| La Liga | 75.2% |
| Ligue 1 | 70.6% |

## Entry Criteria
- At least one defensive team
- Both teams' xG < 1.5
- Not a must-win match
- Odds > 1.40

## Best Situations
- Mid-table clashes
- Teams with nothing to play for
- Rainy conditions
