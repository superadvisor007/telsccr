# Pattern: Title Decider Matches

## Characteristics
- Can go either way: cagey or explosive
- Home team usually favored
- Late goals common (tension release)

## Betting Approach
- Live betting preferred
- Goal after 75' often hits
- Avoid pre-match winner market

## Historical Analysis
- Champions win title deciders 60% at home
- Draws common (25%) - tactical caution
