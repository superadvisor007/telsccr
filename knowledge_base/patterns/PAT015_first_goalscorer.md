# Pattern: First Goalscorer Value

## Expected Hit Rate: 15-25%
## Optimal Odds: 4.00-8.00

## High Value Picks
- Penalty takers (inflated odds often)
- Set-piece specialists
- Players on hot streaks
- Players vs former clubs

## Avoid
- Defenders (unless CB scores often)
- Players who score late usually
- Injured players returning
