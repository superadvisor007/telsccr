# Pattern: Goal After 75th Minute

## Win Rate: 70-75%
## Optimal Odds: 1.45-1.60

## Logic
- 25% of all goals in final 15 minutes
- Tired defenses make mistakes
- Teams chasing push forward
- Substitutes often impact

## Entry Criteria
- At least one team known for late goals
- Competitive match (not dead rubber)
- Both teams capable of scoring

## Teams Known for Late Goals
- Real Madrid
- Manchester United
- Liverpool
- Bayern Munich
