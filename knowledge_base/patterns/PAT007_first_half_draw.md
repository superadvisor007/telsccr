# Pattern: First Half Draw

## Win Rate: 40-45%
## Optimal Odds: 2.10-2.40

## Logic
- Teams start cautiously
- 35% of goals scored in first half
- Draws common as teams "feel out" opponent

## Best Situations
- Derby matches (cautious starts)
- Cup finals
- High-pressure matches
- Cold weather (slower pace)

## Avoid When
- One team on hot streak
- Heavy favorites (often score early)
- Both teams attack-minded managers
