# Pattern: Away Team Scores

## Win Rate: 68-72%
## Optimal Odds: 1.35-1.50

## Logic
- Away teams score in 70% of matches
- Counter-attack opportunities
- Pressure on home team to attack

## Entry Criteria
- Away team scored in last 4 away games
- Home team conceded in last 3 home games
- Odds > 1.35

## Best Value
- When away team is strong but underdog
- When home team has leaky defense
