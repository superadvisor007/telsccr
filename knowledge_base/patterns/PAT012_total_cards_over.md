# Pattern: Over 3.5 Cards

## Win Rate: 60-65%
## Optimal Odds: 1.70-1.90

## Entry Criteria
- Referee averages 4+ cards/game
- Rivalry match or derby
- Physical teams involved
- Neither team with nothing to play for

## High Card Matches
- Local derbies
- Relegation battles
- Title deciders
- Cup knockout matches

## Referee Research Essential
- Check last 10 matches
- Note average yellow and red cards
