# Pattern: Halftime Leader Wins (Live)

## Win Rate: 80-85%
## Optimal Odds: 1.20-1.35

## Logic
- Teams leading at HT win 80%+ of matches
- Psychological advantage
- Can play counter-attack second half

## Live Betting Strategy
- Wait for HT leader
- Back at odds > 1.25
- Better value if favorite is losing

## Caution
- 2+ goal lead almost certain (95%+)
- 1 goal lead less reliable (78%)
