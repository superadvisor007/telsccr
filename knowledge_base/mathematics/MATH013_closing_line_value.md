# Closing Line Value (CLV)

## What is CLV
- Comparing your bet price to closing odds
- Consistent CLV = long-term profit

## How to Track
Opening odds: 1.90
Your bet: 1.90
Closing odds: 1.80
CLV = (1.90 - 1.80) / 1.80 = +5.6%

## Target CLV
- >2% CLV = sustainable edge
- Track over 500+ bets minimum
