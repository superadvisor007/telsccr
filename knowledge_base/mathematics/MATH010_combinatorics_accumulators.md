# Accumulator Mathematics

## Combined Probability
P(all win) = P1 × P2 × P3 × ...

Example: 3-fold at 70% each
P = 0.70 × 0.70 × 0.70 = 34.3%

## Optimal Accumulator Size
| Legs | Break-even Strike Rate |
|------|------------------------|
| 2 | 64% per leg |
| 3 | 70% per leg |
| 4 | 74% per leg |
| 5 | 76% per leg |

## EV Decay
Each leg adds variance and reduces practical EV
Recommendation: Max 3-4 legs for serious betting
