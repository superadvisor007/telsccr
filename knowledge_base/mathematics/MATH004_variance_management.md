# Variance & Bankroll Management

## Standard Deviation in Betting
σ = √(n × p × (1-p))

For 100 bets at 55% win rate:
σ = √(100 × 0.55 × 0.45) = 4.97

## Confidence Intervals
- 68% of results within ±1σ
- 95% of results within ±2σ
- 99.7% of results within ±3σ

## Bankroll Requirements
| Win Rate | Recommended Bankroll |
|----------|---------------------|
| 52% | 100 units |
| 55% | 75 units |
| 58% | 50 units |
| 60%+ | 40 units |

## Drawdown Expectations
- 10% drawdown: 70% probability over 100 bets
- 20% drawdown: 35% probability
- 30% drawdown: 15% probability
