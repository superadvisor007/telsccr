# Drawdown Management

## What is Drawdown
- Peak to trough decline
- Measured in units or %

## Expected Drawdowns
| Win Rate | Expected Max DD |
|----------|-----------------|
| 52% | 25-30% |
| 55% | 20-25% |
| 58% | 15-20% |

## Recovery Formula
From 20% drawdown, need +25% to recover
From 50% drawdown, need +100% to recover
