# Christmas/New Year Period

## Fixture Density
- Up to 4 matches in 10 days
- No other major league does this
- Squad depth crucial

## Betting Approach
- Back teams with 25+ player squads
- Fade teams with thin squads
- Injury rates spike +30%

## Boxing Day Patterns
- Often high-scoring (festive atmosphere)
- Upsets more common
- Players' minds on holidays
