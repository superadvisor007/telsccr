# Kickoff Time Patterns

## Saturday 3pm (Traditional)
- Baseline performance
- Best atmosphere
- Normal preparation time

## Early Kickoff (12:30-13:30)
- Away teams struggle more
- -0.1 xG for away team
- Body clock not optimal

## Late Kickoff (20:00+)
- Better atmosphere
- Home advantage increased
- +0.1 xG for home team

## Sunday/Monday Night
- Recovery time important
- Check Wednesday matches
- Potential letdown spot
