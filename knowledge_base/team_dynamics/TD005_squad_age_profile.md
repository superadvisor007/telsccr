# Squad Age Analysis

## Optimal Age Profile
- Average age 26-28 years
- Mix of experience and athleticism
- Peak performance window

## Young Squads (<25 avg)
- Inconsistent results
- High variance in betting
- Better second half of season

## Old Squads (>29 avg)
- Fixture congestion issues
- Strong in single matches
- Fade in 3 games/week periods
