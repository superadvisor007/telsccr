# Squad Harmony Impact

## Indicators of Good Harmony
- Celebrations together
- Social media positivity
- Press conference unity
- Long-serving players

## Indicators of Discord
- Agent activity in press
- Players not celebrating goals
- Manager criticism of individuals
- Training ground incidents

## Performance Impact
- Unified squads: +8% performance
- Divided squads: -15% performance

## Betting Application
- Monitor news for dressing room issues
- Fade teams with known problems
