# Match Fixing Warning Signs

## Betting Market Red Flags
- Sudden odds movement without news
- Unusual volume on specific outcomes
- Asian handicap line moving opposite to money
- Late heavy betting on unlikely outcomes

## On-Field Indicators
- Unusual errors by specific players
- Goalkeeper mistakes in specific moments
- Penalty area "accidents"
- Strange substitution patterns

## High-Risk Matches
| Type | Risk Level |
|------|------------|
| Friendlies | Very High |
| Dead rubber group games | High |
| Relegated team end of season | High |
| Lower league matches | Medium-High |
| Top league, top stakes | Low |

## Protection Strategy
- Avoid matches with suspicious line movements
- Stick to major leagues and high-stakes matches
- Monitor line movement patterns
