# Referee Corruption Historical Cases

## Notable Cases
- Calciopoli (Italy 2006)
- German referee scandal (2005)
- South Korean K-League (2011)

## Warning Signs
- Consistently favoring specific teams
- Unusual penalty/card patterns
- VAR overrule frequency anomalies

## Current Protections
- VAR reduces individual referee power
- Multiple referee assignments
- Random assignment systems
