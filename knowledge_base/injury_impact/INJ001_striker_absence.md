# Star Striker Absence Impact

## Goal Output Drop
| Player Quality | Team Goals Drop |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Elite (20+ goals) | -35% |
| Good (15-20 goals) | -25% |
| Average (10-15) | -15% |

## Betting Adjustments
- Reduce Over bets
- Consider BTTS No
- Under 2.5 more likely

## Recovery Time
- Replacement needs 2-3 games to find form
- Unless backup is also quality
