# Teams That Struggle Away

## Common Characteristics
- Rely heavily on home crowd
- Possession-based but slow
- Poor defensive record
- Young/inexperienced players

## Historically Poor Away Records
- Newly promoted teams
- Teams from smaller cities
- Teams with old/slow squads

## Betting Opportunities
- Lay these teams away
- Back home team win
- BTTS often value (they concede)
