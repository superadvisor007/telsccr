# Neutral Venue Dynamics

## Cup Final Patterns
- Favorites win 55% (down from 60% at home)
- Underdogs perform better (no hostile crowd)
- Extra time more common (25% vs 15%)

## One-Leg Knockout at Neutral
- Higher variance than home/away legs
- Late goals more common
- Cards markets profitable (tension)

## Super Cup/Community Shield
- Often dead rubber energy
- Heavy rotation common
- Unpredictable outcomes
