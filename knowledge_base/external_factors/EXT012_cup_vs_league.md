# Cup Competition Dynamics

## FA Cup/DFB Pokal Patterns
- Giant killings: 8% of matches
- Lower league teams at home: +25% performance
- Premier League teams rotate: -15% strength

## Champions League Intensity
- Group stage: Teams protect themselves
- Knockout: Maximum intensity
- Dead rubbers: Heavy rotation

## Europa League
- Often deprioritized by top teams
- Thursday-Sunday turnaround problematic
- Conference League even more deprioritized
