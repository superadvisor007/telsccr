# Time & Scheduling Impact

## Kickoff Time Effects
| Time | Impact |
|------|--------|
| 12:30 (Early) | -3% performance (body clock) |
| 15:00 (Standard) | Baseline |
| 17:30 (Late) | +2% for home team |
| 20:00+ (Night) | +5% atmosphere effect |

## Monday Night Football
- Teams coming off Sunday games disadvantaged
- 4-day rest vs 3-day rest matters

## Christmas Period (Dec 26 - Jan 2)
- Fixture congestion extreme
- Squad depth crucial
- Injury rates +25%
