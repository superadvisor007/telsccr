# Temperature Effects

## Cold Weather (<5°C / 41°F)
- Muscle injuries +15%
- Passing accuracy -5%
- Sprint speed -3%
- Favors: Physical teams

## Hot Weather (>30°C / 86°F)
- Second half intensity -20%
- Goals in 2nd half drop
- Favors: Technical, patient teams
- Water breaks disrupt rhythm

## Optimal Temperature
- 12-18°C (54-64°F) = peak performance
- No statistical adjustment needed
