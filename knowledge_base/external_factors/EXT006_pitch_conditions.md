# Pitch Quality Impact

## Pitch Types
| Surface | Goal Rate | Style Favored |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| Perfect | Baseline | Technical |
| Good | +2% | Any |
| Poor/Muddy | +8% | Physical |
| Artificial | +5% | Any |

## Known Difficult Pitches
- Relegation-threatened teams often have poor pitches
- February/March = worst conditions in Europe
- Newly promoted teams often upgrade late

## Betting Application
- Check pitch reports before major matches
- Favor physical teams on poor surfaces
