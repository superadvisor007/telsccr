# Wind Impact on Soccer

## Threshold Effects
| Wind Speed | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| <15 mph | Negligible |
| 15-25 mph | Moderate (+0.2 goals) |
| 25-35 mph | Significant (+0.4 goals) |
| >35 mph | Game often postponed |

## Playing Styles
- Long ball teams: severely affected
- Ground passing teams: less affected
- Set pieces become unpredictable

## Betting Adjustments
- Avoid complex markets in high wind
- Favor compact, ground-passing teams
- Goalscorer markets unreliable
