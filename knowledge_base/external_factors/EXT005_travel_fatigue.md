# Travel Distance Impact

## European Competition Travel
| Distance | Performance Impact |
|----------|-------------------|
| <500km | Negligible |
| 500-1500km | -3% |
| 1500-3000km | -7% |
| >3000km | -12% |

## Time Zone Crossing
- 1-2 zones: Minimal impact
- 3-4 zones: -5% performance
- 5+ zones: -10% performance, needs 2-3 days adaptation

## Jet Lag Recovery
- 1 day per time zone crossed
- Eastward travel harder than westward
