# Rain Impact on Soccer Matches

## Statistical Impact
- Light rain: +0.1 goals (slippery, more errors)
- Heavy rain: +0.3 goals (chaos, turnovers)
- Monsoon conditions: Unpredictable, avoid betting

## Team Style Impact
| Style | Rain Effect |
|-------|-------------|
| Possession-based | -15% effectiveness |
| Counter-attack | +10% effectiveness |
| Long-ball | +5% effectiveness |
| High press | -8% effectiveness |

## Pitch Conditions
- Waterlogged pitch = more goals usually
- Technical teams suffer most
- Physical teams benefit
