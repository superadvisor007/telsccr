# International Break Impact

## Post-Break Performance
| Scenario | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| Players not called up | +5% (rest) |
| Players traveled far | -8% (fatigue) |
| Players picked up injuries | -15% |
| National team success | +3% (confidence) |

## First Match Back
- Results more unpredictable
- Form "resets" partially
- New signings often integrated

## Key Statistics
- Home teams win 48% post-break (vs 46% normal)
- Under 2.5 goals hits 53% (vs 48% normal)
