# Altitude Impact in Soccer

## High Altitude Venues (>2000m)
- La Paz, Bolivia: 3,640m
- Mexico City: 2,240m
- Quito, Ecuador: 2,850m

## Performance Impact
| Altitude | Fatigue Increase | Ball Travel |
|----------|------------------|-------------|
| 1000m | +5% | +3% |
| 2000m | +15% | +8% |
| 3000m+ | +30% | +15% |

## Betting Implications
- Home team massive advantage
- Away teams from sea level: -25% performance
- Second half collapse common for visitors
