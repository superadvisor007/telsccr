# Choking Under Pressure

## Definition
- Significant performance drop in crucial moments
- Often linked to overthinking

## Risk Factors
- Young players in first finals
- Players with previous choke history
- High media attention

## Statistical Evidence
| Situation | Choke Risk |
|-----------|------------|
| Title decider | 25% |
| Penalty shootout | 30% |
| Cup final | 22% |
