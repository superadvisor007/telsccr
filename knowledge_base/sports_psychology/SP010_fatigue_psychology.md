# Mental Fatigue Impact

## Causes
- 3+ games in 7 days
- Long travel (4+ hours)
- Emotional matches (derby, cup final)
- International duty

## Performance Drop
| Fatigue Level | Performance Impact |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Mild | -5% |
| Moderate | -12% |
| Severe | -20% |

## Recovery Times
- Physical: 72 hours minimum
- Mental: 96 hours after emotional games
- Combined: 5-7 days for full recovery
