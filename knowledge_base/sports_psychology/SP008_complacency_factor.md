# Complacency in Favorites

## Risk Situations
- Huge favorites (odds <1.20)
- Already qualified/safe in table
- Weaker opponent perception
- After big Champions League win

## Historical Upset Patterns
- 18% of matches with odds <1.25 don't end in favorite win
- Post-European matches: upset rate increases 25%

## Betting Strategy
- Avoid laying heavy favorites blindly
- Look for complacency indicators
- Double chance value on underdogs
