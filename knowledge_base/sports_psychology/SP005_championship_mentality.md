# Championship Mentality

## Elite Team Characteristics
- Win 75%+ of matches after going behind
- Score 35%+ goals in final 15 minutes
- Maintain performance under fixture congestion

## Teams with Proven Mentality (2020-2024)
- Real Madrid (Champions League specialists)
- Manchester City (domestic dominance)
- Bayern Munich (Bundesliga control)
- Liverpool (comeback specialists)

## Betting Implication
- Back elite teams in close matches
- Late goal markets favorable for mentality teams
