# Clutch Performance Analysis

## What is Clutch
- Performing above average in high-pressure moments
- Statistical rarity (only 5-10% of players)

## Clutch Indicators
| Metric | Clutch Player |
|--------|---------------|
| Goals in final 10 min | >30% of total |
| Big match performance | +15% vs baseline |
| Penalty conversion | >85% |

## Betting Application
- Back teams with clutch players in tight games
- Value in "goal after 75'" markets
