# Pressure Handling by Position

## Goalkeeper Psychology
- Penalty saves: confidence builds exponentially
- Errors: require 3-4 clean sheets to recover mentally
- Age 28-32 = peak pressure handling

## Defender Psychology
- Individual errors most psychologically damaging
- Recovery time: 2-3 matches
- Partnership stability crucial

## Forward Psychology
- Goal drought affects confidence after 5+ games
- One goal breaks drought = +25% next match scoring prob
