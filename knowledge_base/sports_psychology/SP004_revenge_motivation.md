# Revenge Motivation Factor

## When It Applies
- Heavy defeat in reverse fixture (3+ goals)
- Controversial loss (refereeing error)
- Player returning to former club
- Manager facing former club

## Statistical Impact
- Teams seeking revenge: +0.15 xG
- Player revenge (facing ex-club): +12% performance boost
- Effect diminishes after 2 seasons

## Betting Application
- Check H2H for heavy defeats
- Monitor player transfers between clubs
- Manager history important
