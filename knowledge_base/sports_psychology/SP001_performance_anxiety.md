# Performance Anxiety in Soccer

## Impact on Betting
- Players with anxiety underperform by 15-25% in high-pressure matches
- Penalty conversion drops from 78% to 62% under extreme pressure
- Young players (<23) more susceptible than veterans

## Betting Adjustments
| Situation | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| Title decider | -0.3 xG for nervous team |
| Relegation battle | -0.2 xG for relegation-threatened |
| Cup final debut | -15% performance for inexperienced |

## Key Indicators
- Social media silence before big matches = high anxiety
- Press conference body language analysis
- Previous performance in similar situations
