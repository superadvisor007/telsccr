## âš½ Main Betting Markets

### 1. OVER/UNDER GOALS

#### Over 1.5 Goals (Mindestens 2 Tore)
- **Description**: Match MUST have 2+ total goals
- **Examples**: 2-0, 1-1, 3-1, 2-2 = WIN | 1-0, 0-0, 0-1 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 1.20 - 1.50
- **Typical Probability**: 70-80%
- **Best for**: High-scoring teams (Bayern, Dortmund, Liverpool)
- **âš ï¸ KEY**: This is the MOST COMMON low-odds bet

#### Under 1.5 Goals (Maximal 1 Tor)
- **Description**: Match MUST have 0 or 1 total goals ONLY
- **Examples**: 0-0, 1-0, 0-1 = WIN | 2-0, 1-1, 3-0 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 2.50 - 5.00 (HIGH ODDS - RARE!)
- **Typical Probability**: 15-30%
- **Best for**: Very defensive teams, bad weather, low-quality teams
- **âš ï¸ CRITICAL**: Under 1.5 with 99.8% probability is ABSURD!
  - In Bundesliga, average is ~3 goals per match
  - Under 1.5 happens only ~20% of the time
  - **NEVER predict Under 1.5 with >30% confidence**

#### Over 2.5 Goals (Mindestens 3 Tore)
- **Description**: Match MUST have 3+ total goals
- **Examples**: 3-0, 2-1, 4-1, 2-2 = WIN | 1-1, 2-0, 1-0 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 1.50 - 2.00
- **Typical Probability**: 50-65%
- **Best for**: Attacking teams, open games, Bundesliga

#### Under 2.5 Goals (Maximal 2 Tore)
- **Description**: Match MUST have 2 or fewer total goals
- **Examples**: 0-0, 1-0, 1-1, 2-0 = WIN | 3-0, 2-1, 4-2 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 1.60 - 2.20
- **Typical Probability**: 40-55%
- **Best for**: Defensive teams, Serie A, tight matches

#### Over 3.5 Goals (Mindestens 4 Tore)
- **Description**: Match MUST have 4+ total goals
- **Examples**: 4-0, 3-1, 2-2, 5-1 = WIN | 2-1, 3-0, 1-1 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 2.00 - 3.50
- **Typical Probability**: 30-45%
- **Best for**: Very attacking teams, end of season, derby matches

---

### 2. BOTH TEAMS TO SCORE (BTTS)

#### BTTS Yes (Beide Teams treffen)
- **Description**: BOTH teams must score at least 1 goal
- **Examples**: 1-1, 2-1, 3-2, 1-2 = WIN | 0-0, 3-0, 0-2 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 1.50 - 2.00
- **Typical Probability**: 50-65%
- **Best for**: Open games, attacking teams, poor defenses

#### BTTS No (Ein oder beide Teams ohne Tor)
- **Description**: At least ONE team scores 0 goals
- **Examples**: 0-0, 2-0, 0-3 = WIN | 1-1, 2-1, 3-2 = LOSS
- **Typical Odds**: 1.70 - 2.50
- **Typical Probability**: 35-50%
- **Best for**: Strong defenses, one-sided matches

---

### 3. MATCH RESULT (1X2)

#### Home Win (1)
- **Description**: Home team wins
- **Typical Odds**: 1.30 - 3.00 (depends on favorite)
- **Typical Probability**: 35-75%

#### Draw (X)
- **Description**: Match ends tied
- **Typical Odds**: 3.00 - 4.50
- **Typical Probability**: 20-30%

#### Away Win (2)
- **Description**: Away team wins
- **Typical Odds**: 1.50 - 5.00 (depends on underdog)
- **Typical Probability**: 20-60%

---

### 4. DOUBLE CHANCE

#### 1X (Home Win or Draw)
- **Description**: Home team doesn't lose
- **Typical Odds**: 1.10 - 1.40
- **Typical Probability**: 65-85%
- **Best for**: Strong favorites at home

#### 12 (Home Win or Away Win)
- **Description**: No draw
- **Typical Odds**: 1.20 - 1.60
- **Typical Probability**: 70-80%
- **Best for**: Attacking teams, high-scoring games

#### X2 (Draw or Away Win)
- **Description**: Home team doesn't win
- **Typical Odds**: 1.40 - 2.00
- **Typical Probability**: 50-70%
- **Best for**: Away favorites or balanced matches

---

### 5. ASIAN HANDICAP

#### Asian Handicap -0.5 (Team must win)
- Same as Match Win but with different odds calculation

#### Asian Handicap -1.5 (Team must win by 2+ goals)
- **Examples**: 2-0, 3-0, 3-1 = WIN | 1-0, 2-1 = LOSS

#### Asian Handicap +1.5 (Team can't lose by 2+)
- **Examples**: 0-0, 0-1, 1-2 = WIN | 0-2, 1-3 = LOSS
